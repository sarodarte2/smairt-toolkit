# Prompts

AI prompts, session logs, and intellectual contribution tracking.

## Files

- `AI_CONTEXT.md` - Give this to your AI: explains its role and the SMAIRT workflow
- `CODE_CONVENTIONS.md` - Give this to your AI: how to format code output
- `KNOWN_PATTERNS.md` - Give this to your AI: reusable code patterns and common error fixes
- `SESSION_START.md` - Ready-to-paste prompts for starting new AI sessions
- `00_priming_prompts.md` - Prompts to initialize your AI session
- `session_log.md` - Running log of all prompts and responses
- `intellectual_contribution.md` - Track where YOU made critical steps

## Purpose

Record your prompts in a separate file—this provides a track record of all the things you've asked AI. It also records what your intellectual contribution to the effort is.

## Priming Prompts

`AI_CONTEXT.md`, `CODE_CONVENTIONS.md`, and `KNOWN_PATTERNS.md` provide prompts for the AI behavior during the session.

## Known Patterns & Error Prevention

`KNOWN_PATTERNS.md` is a **living reference** that you update as you work. It records:

1. **Reusable code patterns** — working snippets that should be reused across scripts
2. **Common errors & fixes** — mistakes that have been solved (so AI doesn't repeat them)
3. **Environment configuration** — paths, versions, platform quirks
4. **Consistency rules** — standards enforced across all scripts
5. **Anti-patterns** — approaches that were tried and failed

**Update this file after each iteration** when you encounter new errors or develop reusable patterns. Feed it to your AI at every session start.
