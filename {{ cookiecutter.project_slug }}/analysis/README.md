# Analysis

Interpretation of results and future directions.

## The 4-Part Structure

"You have the hypothesis, the experiment, and the results, and then the interpretation."

## Files

- `iteration_log.md` - Interpretation of each iteration's results through the lens of the hypothesis
- `future_directions.md` - What to try next (feeds back into background for next iteration)

## What Goes Here

1. Did results support the hypothesis?
2. What limitations were identified?
3. What secondary observations were made?
4. What are the logical next steps?

"The future directions lead right back into that background section."
