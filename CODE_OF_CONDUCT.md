# Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation welcoming and harassment-free. Be respectful, evaluate evidence
constructively, acknowledge uncertainty, protect confidential research material, and prioritize
community health and safety.

Harassment, intimidation, discriminatory language, unwanted sexual attention, doxxing, and
publishing private information are unacceptable. Report concerns privately through the
organization's maintainer contact route. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict
participation, or ban participants when behavior is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
