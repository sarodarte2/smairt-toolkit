# Terminal easter-egg attributions

SMAIRT's optional terminal marks are compact, hand-refined ASCII approximations included for fun.
They are not official artwork, are disabled by default, and never replace the SMAIRT wordmark.

- The PNNL name and associated marks belong to Pacific Northwest National Laboratory and their
  respective rights holders.
- The UTEP name, Miner identity, and associated marks belong to The University of Texas at El Paso
  and their respective rights holders.

Institutional names and marks belong to their respective institutions. These terminal
approximations are unofficial easter eggs and do not imply endorsement. SMAIRT does not redistribute
the raster reference images used to guide the approximations.
